# MeetingSpeak v3 — Result

## What changed and why

### MeetingSpeak.js
- Added `sports` to `TONE_MODES`
- Added sports tone handling in `toneWrap`: errors get "Flag on the play. Review the tape." and logs get `[play-by-play]` prefix

### demo/app.js (full rewrite of demo logic)

**New data and constants:**
- `SPORTS_CELEBRATIONS` — 12 randomized sports celebration toasts shown when advancing a status (TOUCHDOWN, SLAM DUNK, HOME RUN, GOAL, HOLE IN ONE, etc.)
- `CORPORATE_BUZZWORDS` — 12 randomized corporate buzzword phrases that can be injected into the executive summary field
- New default watchlist: Succession, Ted Lasso, Industry, The Wire, Abbott Elementary — all with corporate/sports-flavored descriptions
- `TRAILER_LINES` expanded with 14 sports/corporate lines (e.g. "A full-court press on your emotional bandwidth.", "Delivers ROI on your couch time.")

**New features:**
- `getSynergyScore()` — computes a fake "Synergy Score" (0–99%) from watching/watched/queued ratio
- `getSynergyLabel()` — rates the score: MARKET-LEADING / HIGH-VELOCITY / ON-TRACK / NEEDS ALIGNMENT / PIPELINE RISK
- **5th hero stat**: "Synergy Score" displayed with visual differentiation (green accent)
- **"Inject buzzword" button** — fires `injectBuzzword()` which randomly fills the executive summary field with corporate speak and shows a toast
- `getRandomSportsCelebration()` — called on every status advance instead of the plain "Status advanced." toast
- Activity feed titles now rotate through corporate hustle verbs: "Pivoted the board.", "Moved the ball forward.", "Synergy event logged.", etc.

**Copy overhaul:**
- Status labels: Queued → "Pre-Game", Watching → "Crushing It", Watched → "Win Column"
- Form labels: "Title" → "Content deliverable", "Service" → "Vendor platform", "Status" → "Pipeline stage", "Trailer line" → "Executive summary"
- Card actions: "Advance status" → "Advance the ball", "Edit" → "Renegotiate", "Delete" → "Cut from roster"
- "Added" timestamp → "Onboarded"
- Hero stats labels: "Titles tracked" → "Total pipeline", "Queued next" → "Pre-game queue", "Currently watching" → "Actively crushing", "Closed out" → "Win column"
- Board heading: "Leverage your content pipeline. Advance the ball. Iterate. Win."
- Toast messages throughout: "Let's move the ball.", "Cap space freed up.", "Stakeholders notified.", "The algorithm has spoken.", etc.
- Recommendations now include a `fitLabel` (HIGH PRIORITY / STRATEGIC FIT / EARNED / DEVELOPMENT PICK)

## Assumptions made
- The task wanted the demo layer upgraded, not a new folder — v3 replaces v2's demo files in place
- "Funnier" meant more committed to the bit: more consistent corporate-sports crossover, not just more buzzwords sprinkled in
- The synergy score is intentionally a fake metric (always looks plausible, never means anything)
- Sports celebrations rotate randomly so advancing the same card twice gives different results
